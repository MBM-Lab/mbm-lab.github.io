cv: "Enia Ottavi is a research fellow at the University of Trento. In 2024, she received her Master's degree in Mathematics from the University of Trento, Italy, specializing in mathematical modeling for biomedical applications. Her Master's thesis, \"Rule-Based Modeling of Cardiac Fibers in Complex Atrial Geometries with Applications to Electrophysiological Simulations,\" explored methods for reconstructing fiber distributions in atrial geometries with a focus on electrophysiological simulations. She is currently working with the openCARP platform."
