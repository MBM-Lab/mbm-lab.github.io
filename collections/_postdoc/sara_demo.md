cv: "Sara Demo is a junior researcher in mathematics at the University of Trento. In 2024, she obtained the MSc in Mathematics from the University of Trento, with a thesis on numerical methods for hyperbolic PDEs. The Master provided to her a solid background in applied mathematics and advanced modeling techniques for biomedical problems. She is particularly interested in the field of modeling, mathematical and numerical analysis of PDEs, with a focus on addressing critical challenges in healthcare. Currently, she is involved in developing coupling strategies for combining the bidomain model with its non-homogenized counterpart EMI model for the MICROCARD project."
