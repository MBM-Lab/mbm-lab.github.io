cv: "Camilla Pera is a research fellow at the University of Trento. In 2024, she received her master's degree in Mathematics from the University of Trento, with a focus on biomedical applications. In her master thesis, entitled \"Towards a Sex-Specific Action Potential Model for Rabbit Atrial Cells: Mathematical Modeling and Analysis\", she extended an action potential model for rabbit atrial cells by incorporating sex-specific differences based on experimental data. She is currently working on the development of a single-cell atrial model using the EMI framework."
